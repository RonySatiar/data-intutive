Our Mission

At Data Intuitive, we are driven by a passion to improve health and well-being through innovative solutions in biotech computational R&D as well as ensuring the prosperity of the DI team. More than just consultancy for the biotech R&D industry, we contribute to enhancing the quality of life.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading innovator in supporting biotech computational R&D, with a keen focus on refining data workflow approach, emphasizing long-term optimization.

Open Source

To further support this mission and vision, we strategically divide our operations between non-profit and profit sectors. This division allows us to more effectively support the scientific community. We are dedicated to developing open-source tools and resources, believing that such contributions are crucial for nurturing a collaborative and forward-moving scientific environment. Our open-source initiatives are not just products; they are part of our broader commitment to sharing knowledge and tools that can accelerate scientific discovery and healthcare improvements.

Our Commitment

Our commitment is to enable faster, more cost-effective experimentation and data interpretation. By streamlining biotech data workflows, we aim to accelerate the timeline for bringing new medicines to market

Our journey

With the start of the development of Viash in 2019, Data Intuitive began a new chapter in its history. The founders, Toni, Andy, and Robrecht, bring a wealth of expertise in consultancy, data science, bioinformatics, and full-stack IT, totaling over 50 years of experience from operations to strategic projects.

Our Team

Comprising seasoned IT professionals, data scientists and computanional biologists, our team masters the technologies involved in data workflows, data science and bioinformatics. Collaborating closely with our clients, we live out our flexibility and solution-focused mindset by creating tailored strategies, deploying the latest technologies, and ensuring project success through through consistent support.

This harmonious blend of experience and innovation underscores our commitment to delivering exceptional service and pioneering projects and solutions that rise above conventional consultancy tasks.

We are driven by a passion to improve health and well-being.

Data Intuitive’s Key Values

Innovation

We cultivate an innovative culture that fosters a fast-paced and dynamic environment. This encourages our IT experts to constantly explore and challenge the boundaries of what is possible.

Co-Creation

We collaborate with companies on both short and long-term projects. Our partnership approach emphasizes driving innovation and growth through data-driven methods, rather than just supplying temporary staff.

Sustainability

We are committed to promoting sustainability in all aspects of our operations. This includes utilizing open-source software, fostering a culture of work-life balance, and implementing the use of electric cars.
